Ques:4  Write a short note on a tagand element with an example?

Ans:     Tag:- This is known as reserved word and keyword. Which is made to do special work.It is displayed by angular brackets(<>).
                     <> - open tag
                     </>- close tag
                     Example: <p>,<br>,<h1>
         Element:-Tag whatever content is between the start tag and close tag and ending tag. That statement are called element.
                     Example: <i>This is italic tag</i>
